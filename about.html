About Us

SS Bags India is committed to producing unisex bags for very need to suit men, women and kids. Catering to the rural and urban parts of Coimbatore, these bags are designed and developed within a 5000 sq feet area with 2 floors dedicated solely for the machinery. SS Bags has its very own in-house manufacturing units that caters to all its production needs. 20 ft machines are dedicated exclusively for stitching along with several hand-cutting, laser cutting and embroidery machines.

Our current marketing front is within the ecommerce realm and in covering a distribution setup within the boundaries of India. We at SS Bags India intends to do so with the help of retail chains and exclusive stores across Tamil Nadu in both the online and offline mode. We own the pride of manufacturing bags that help increase and elevate the bag production in the south which is immensely scarce and design bags that can cater to almost every daily need.
